Our Story

Nestled in the heart of Chișinău, Bonjour Cafe was born from a simple love for great coffee and genuine hospitality. Since opening our doors, we have been committed to sourcing the finest single-origin beans and crafting each cup with care.

Our cozy space is designed for everyone — whether you're catching up with friends, working on your next big idea, or simply savoring a quiet moment. We believe every cup tells a story, and we'd love to be part of yours.
